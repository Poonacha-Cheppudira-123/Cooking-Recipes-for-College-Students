Grilled Cheese and Tomato Soup: Tomato Soup Recipe: Tools: stove and pot Ingredients: tomatoes, onion, garlic, and butter Step 1: melt 2 tablespoons of butter in pot Step 2: add ½ teaspoons garlic and ⅓ tablespoons onion to butter Step 3: add 6-7 fresh diced tomatoes to pot Step 4: simmer on a low heat until the tomatoes soften, about 8 to 10 minutes Step 5: stir occasionally until puree and do not boil
