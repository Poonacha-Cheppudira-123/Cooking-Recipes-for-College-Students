Avocado Toast: Recipe: Tools: knife, large spoon, toaster Ingredients: two whole avocados, bread Step 1: split avocados in half from top to bottom (vertically) around seed Step 2: scoop of bright green part and throw away the rest Step 3: cut remaining halves into desired piece sizes Step 4: toast bread Step 5: put slices on toast and enjoy

Estimated Cooking Time: 25 minutes

Estimated Cost: $1.5-3.15 per avocado $1.52-1.95 per loaf
